Research

The average person changes careers 5 to 7 times in their work life.

30% of the workforce will change careers every year.

The job switching rate has nearly doubled in the last 10 years.

Problem Statement

According to Careers Online advice, the average person changes careers 5-7 times during their work life and 30% of the workforce will change careers every 12 months. Through interviews and surveys, we discovered that profesionals switching careers have trouble finding the time to do a thorough search for a mentor, knowing where to start that process and keying in on the right person for them.

How might we provide a solution that allows users to find the right, long-term mentor quickly and easily from the start?
